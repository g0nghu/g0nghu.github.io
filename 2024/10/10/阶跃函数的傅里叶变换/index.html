lim⁡a→0+fa(t)=θ(t)\lim_{a\rightarrow0^+}f_a(t) = \theta(t) a→0+lim​fa​(t)=θ(t)

F[θ(t)]=lim⁡a→0+F[fa(t)]=lim⁡a→0+1a+iω\mathcal{F}[\theta(t)] = \lim_{a\rightarrow0^+}\mathcal{F[f_a(t)]} = \lim_{a\rightarrow0^+}\frac{1}{a+i\omega} F[θ(t)]=a→0+lim​F[fa​(t)]=a→0+lim​a+iω1​
